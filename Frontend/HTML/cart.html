My Coupon


Ah, you haven't obtained any coupons yet. Get started browsing through our delicious deals now and take advantage of the attractive offers!



How Its Works

  1. Click on "View LiveCoupon" to use the digital coupon when you are at the shop. Inform merchant that you have a KindMeal.my LiveCoupon, and use it during your payment.
  2. For shops that accept Print Coupons, you will see a "Print Coupon" function available for the coupon. Print it out, inform the shop, and use it during your payment. Some shops may have a usage delay for such coupons
  3. Once a coupon expires, you can no longer use it. Be sure to make use of it before the expiry date is reached!
